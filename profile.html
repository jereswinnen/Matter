Matter is the online playground of digital product designer, Jeremy Swinnen.

Over the past few years I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with start-ups and smart people from all over the world.

My name is Jeremy Swinnen, I'm a freelance product designer and creative developer based in Antwerp, Belgium. I specialise in user interface design and frontend web development. I've been interested in design for as long as I can remember and I've been doing this as my career for the past 10 years and, recently, I went freelance full-time.

Through visually pleasing design and modular code, I translate people's ideas into award-winning products. I find incredible value in the ability to code my designs and create interactive prototypes throughout the design process.

My work has been featured numerous times in local and international publications. A few of the apps I designed were also featured by Apple in the App Store.

Skills and services. What I can help you with.

User Interface Design

Do you have a great idea for an iOS or Android app or does your existing app requires a visual overhaul? With a sense for great and usable design, I can give your app the interface it deserves.

Responsive Web Design

I start every new project with a "mobile-first" approach. This ensures that the design works perfectly on the device your visitors visit your website with the most.

Web Development

Using cutting edge web technologies and by writing clean and readable code, I can make interactive prototypes of my designs as well as bring my designs to life.
