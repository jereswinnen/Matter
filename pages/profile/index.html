Me, myself and I

My name is Jeremy Swinnen, I'm a Product Designer for click and tap from Belgium. I design products for screens covering all sizes. Early in my life I knew I wanted to make a living by designing websites & apps. Every facet of design gets me excited, be it those shiny chamfered edges of an iPhone or a nicely constructed building.

My expertise includes grid systems, typography and interface design. I have a good understanding of HTML, CSS & Javascript and know what's possible and what not. Regarding iOS apps, I worked closely together with various developers. One of my recent projects is Diabetik for iOS, which I built with Nial Giacomelli. The app was very well received and we made a lot of people's lives easier. Be sure to check out the case study.

After Diabetik I went to work for Realo, a startup based in Ghent, Belgium. Being the first designer of the team, my role was to define a strong design language that could be applied to a range of products across many different devices.

About this website

This site is an experiment with modern web technologies, colour and typography. I hand-coded this website myself using SASS to write my CSS and vanilla HTML. This site is type-set in the wonderful Libre Franklin by Impallari Type and are being served by Google Fonts.
